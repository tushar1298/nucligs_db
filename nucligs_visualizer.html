NucLigs Database
A Nucleoside and Nucleotide Analog Repository

Exploring the Chemical Universe of Nucleoside & Nucleotide Analogs

NucLigs is a scientifically curated cheminformatics platform integrating validated nucleoside and nucleotide analogs reported across decades of biomedical literature. The platform enables advanced molecular exploration, therapeutic analysis, medicinal chemistry investigations, structure–activity relationship studies, computational screening, and AI-driven drug discovery.
